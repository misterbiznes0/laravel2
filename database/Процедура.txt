В SQLite db browser процедуры хранить нельзя, так что сделала так

SELECT 
    u.name AS customer_name,
    COUNT(b.id) AS total_orders,
    SUM(json_array_length(b.selected_seats)) AS total_tickets,
    SUM(b.total_price) AS total_amount
FROM bookings b
JOIN users u ON u.id = b.user_id
JOIN movie_sessions ms ON ms.id = b.movie_session_id
WHERE datetime(ms.start_time) BETWEEN datetime('2026-05-01') AND datetime('2026-05-31')
GROUP BY u.id;

datetime('2026-05-01') AND datetime('2026-05-31') - это меняется под нужные даты
